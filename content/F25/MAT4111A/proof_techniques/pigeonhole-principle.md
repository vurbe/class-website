# Pigeonhole principle

The pigeonhole principle says that if you have $n$ holes and $n+1$ pigeons living in the holes, then at least one hole has two or more pigeons.

Moreover, if you have $n$ holes and $>kn$ pigeons, then at least one hole has $k$ or more pigeons.
